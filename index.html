Meet the therapist.

Ina Edmands, LMT and LE, studied holistic philosophies and treatments for more than 35 years, near and far. She loves what she does, has worked in local spas for a number of years, and applies her knowledge in treatment and touch. Dr. Hauschka skincare fuses beautifully with her strong desire to treat people in harmony with nature.

I am deeply inspired to work with compassion. I like to bring well-being to my fellow man through nature with natural products. I like sharing the knowledge I have gained over the years regarding how skin works and its close relationship to the lifestyle we all lead.
